3. Java:

4. C++:
A powerful, high-performance language offering low-level memory control, suitable for performance-critical applications.
Widely used in game development (game engines), operating systems, embedded systems, and high-performance computing.
Despite a steep learning curve, its efficiency and control make it indispensable where speed and resource management are paramount.
